How Delivery Works

  • Step 1 – Place Order: Contact us via our website, WhatsApp, or email with your service request and required details.
  • Step 2 – Make Payment: Send payment via bKash or Nagad and share the transaction reference with us.
  • Step 3 – Order Processing: We verify your payment and begin processing your order immediately.
  • Step 4 – Delivery: Your service is delivered to your game account, via WhatsApp message, or via email depending on the service type.
  • Step 5 – Confirmation: We follow up to confirm you've received and can access your order successfully.

Delays & Special Cases

  • Peak Hours: Orders placed during peak hours (evening or weekends) may experience slight delays beyond the standard timeframe.
  • Third-Party Issues: Delays caused by game server outages, platform maintenance, or third-party restrictions are outside our control. We will notify you as soon as possible.
  • Incomplete Information: If your order details are incomplete or incorrect, delivery will be delayed until we receive the correct information from you.
  • Public Holidays: Service availability may be limited on major public holidays in Bangladesh. Orders will still be processed but may take slightly longer.

⚠ Important Before You Order

Make sure to provide accurate order details (Player ID, phone number, email, etc.) at the time of ordering. Errors in provided information that cause delivery issues are the customer's responsibility and are not eligible for a refund. If you're unsure about what information to provide, contact us first before making payment.

Didn't Receive Your Order?

If your order has not arrived within the stated delivery timeframe, please contact us immediately. Include your payment transaction reference and order details when reaching out.

  • For gaming top-ups not received within 2 hours of payment — contact us.
  • For premium subscriptions not received within 12 hours — contact us.
  • For web development projects — we provide regular progress updates. Any delays are communicated proactively.
