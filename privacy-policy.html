Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: May 2, 2025

✦ We Respect Your Privacy

RabbiHossainLTD is committed to protecting your personal data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely to process your orders and improve your experience.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, and social media handles when you place an order or contact us.
  • Order Information: Service type, quantity, payment amount, and transaction references.
  • Game/Account Details: Player IDs, in-game usernames, or account IDs required to deliver gaming or digital services.
  • Device & Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, and pages visited — collected automatically via basic analytics to improve our website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders accurately and efficiently.
  • To communicate with you about your order status, issues, or updates.
  • To send important service notifications (we do not send unsolicited marketing emails).
  • To improve our website, services, and overall customer experience.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of transactions.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following limited cases:

  • Service Fulfillment: Minimal data (e.g., Player ID) shared with third-party platforms only as needed to deliver your ordered service.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our business or users.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the new entity under the same privacy protections.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • All sensitive order data is handled securely and not stored longer than necessary.
  • We do not store payment credentials (bKash/Nagad PIN, bank passwords) — only transaction references.
  • Access to customer data is strictly limited to authorized personnel only.

Your Rights

  • Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of any promotional communications at any time by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

To exercise your rights or for any privacy-related concerns, contact us at info@rabbihossainltd.online.

Privacy Concerns?

If you have any questions about how your data is handled, don't hesitate to reach out.
