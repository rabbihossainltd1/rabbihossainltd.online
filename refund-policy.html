Refund Policy

We are committed to your satisfaction. Please read our refund policy carefully before placing any order.

Last updated: May 2, 2025

✦ Our Commitment to You

At RabbiHossainLTD, we take every order seriously. Our goal is to deliver exactly what you pay for — on time and without issues. If something goes wrong on our end, we will make it right.

Eligible Refund Cases

You are eligible for a full or partial refund in the following situations:

  • Service not delivered: If we fail to deliver your order within the promised timeframe and cannot provide an alternative.
  • Wrong service delivered: If you received a different service or product than what you ordered.
  • Technical failure: If the service stopped working within 24 hours of delivery due to our error (not user-side issues).
  • Duplicate payment: If you were charged more than once for the same order.
  • Order cancellation (before processing): If your order has not yet been processed, you may cancel for a full refund.

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Digital goods already delivered: Once a premium subscription, activation code, or login credential has been delivered and confirmed working, it cannot be refunded.
  • Gaming top-ups credited: Free Fire diamonds or other in-game credits that have already been added to your account are non-refundable.
  • Change of mind: We do not accept refunds due to a change of mind after a service has been successfully delivered.
  • User error: Incorrect information provided by the customer (e.g., wrong Player ID, wrong account details) is not eligible for refund.
  • Third-party platform issues: Refunds are not available for issues caused by the platform (e.g., game server downtime, app policy changes).
  • Violation of our Terms: Accounts or orders suspended due to policy violations are ineligible for refunds.

⚠ Important Note on Digital Services

All digital services (Premium Apps, subscriptions, gaming credits) are delivered instantly or within a short window. Due to the nature of digital goods, once delivered and confirmed, refunds are generally not possible. Please verify your order details carefully before completing payment.

Refund Timeframe

  • Refund Request Window: Refund requests must be submitted within 72 hours of order placement or delivery.
  • Processing Time: Once approved, refunds are processed within 3–7 business days.
  • Payment Method: Refunds are returned via the original payment method (bKash, Nagad, or bank transfer as applicable).

How to Request a Refund

To submit a refund request, contact us through any of the following channels with your order details:

  • Email us at info@rabbihossainltd.online with the subject line "Refund Request – [Your Order ID]".
  • Message us on WhatsApp at +880 1731-410341.
  • Reach out via our Facebook page: facebook.com/rabbihossainltd.
  • Include your full name, order date, service name, and reason for the refund request.

Have a Refund Issue?

Our support team typically responds within 2–6 hours. We're here to help resolve any concerns quickly.
